Wage (Salary) Tax

Employers are required to withhold Wage Tax from the employees. The Tax Amount depends on the total monthly income of the employee. There is no tax on salaries below 5,000 AFN.


Business Receipt Tax

Please enter the total amount you have earned this quarter in sales. Businesses should pay 4% of their total sales to the Afghan government every quarter.
